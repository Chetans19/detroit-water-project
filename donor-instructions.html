Thank you for your kind support of the Detroit Water Project. Your contribution will make a huge difference in the life of a Detroit resident.

Here is how to make your contribution directly to the Detroit Water & Sewerage Department:

  1. Visit the Detroit Water & Sewerage Department website and select the Standard Access tab.
  2. Enter as the account number and select Continue.

  3. Select Make Payment. Please donate . This amount is based on your original pledged support.

    Note: A bill is split amongst several donors--so overpaying confuses others who were also assigned to the account. If you would like to provide more support, please add yourself to the donor list again.

  4. Use your email address or support@detroitwaterprojecthelp.zendesk.com in the email field.
  5. You're done! Now spread the word!

Thank you! Now, spread the word with Twitter hashtag #DetroitWater.

Note on scam warnings:

  • We do not handle any money ourselves. We simply match residents with donors. Payments are received directly by the Detroit Water & Sewerage Department.
  • We do not have access to your credit card or banking information. The resident does not have access to any of your payment details. All information is transmitted and stored securely on Detroit Water & Sewerage Department servers.
